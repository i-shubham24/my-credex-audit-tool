# Success Metrics (KPIs)

To evaluate the performance of the AI Stack Auditor, we track:
1. **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of users who start an audit vs. those who submit their email (Target: >15%).
2. **Identified Savings Per Lead:** The average monthly savings discovered. This qualifies the lead for Credex's high-tier consulting services.
3. **PDF Download Rate:** Measures the perceived value of the audit report.
4. **Viral Coefficient:** Tracked via UTM parameters when users share their "Optimized" status on LinkedIn.
