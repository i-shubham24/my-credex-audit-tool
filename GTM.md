# Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Targeted Community Launch
- **Product Hunt:** Launch with a focus on "SaaS Cost Optimization for Founders."
- **Niche Newsletters:** Partner with AI-focused newsletters (e.g., Ben's Bites, The Rundown) to offer the tool as a free resource.

### Phase 2: Direct Outreach
- **LinkedIn Automation:** Identify CTOs and Founders of Series A startups who have recently expanded their engineering teams.
- **Cold Emailing:** Use the identified savings potential ($1,000+/yr) as the primary hook in outreach copy.

### Phase 3: Content Marketing
- **Case Studies:** Publish "Before vs. After" blog posts showing real startups that saved 20% on AI spend using our downgrade guide.
