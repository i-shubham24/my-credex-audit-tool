# Landing Page Copy Strategy

### 1. The Hook (Hero Section)
- **Headline:** "Your AI Stack is Leaking Cash."
- **Sub-headline:** "Identify $1,000s in hidden SaaS waste in 60 seconds. Get a professional-grade audit for your startup, for free."

### 2. The Frictionless Form (SpendForm)
- We use **Minimalist Inputs** (Team Size, Monthly Spend) to maximize conversion. 
- **Psychological Trigger:** Users are more likely to complete a "60-second audit" than a "Financial Consultation."

### 3. The Value Loop (Results)
- We show the **Big Number** first (Total Savings).
- We provide an **Instant Action** (Download PDF) to build trust.
- We end with the **Lead Capture** (Email for the "Full Guide") only after the user has seen the value.
